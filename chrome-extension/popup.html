Arabic Lemmatizer

Select Arabic text on any webpage to see unknown words highlighted in red.

Click on a word to mark it as known/unknown.

Hover over a word to see its lemma parts.

How to use:
  1. Select Arabic text on the page
  2. Unknown words will be highlighted in red
  3. Click a word to toggle its known status
  4. Hover over a word to see its lemma parts
